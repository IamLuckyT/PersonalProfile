Who am I?

My name is Letlhogonolo Mothoagae, I am a student of life who in many ways is still paving his way through the world of technology. business and entrepreneurship. I have a passion for technology and I am always eager to learn new things. I believe that technology has the power to change the world and I want to be a part of that change.

My first love for technology began with video games, I was fascinated by how they were made and how they worked. This led me to learn more about computers and programming, and I quickly fell in love with the world of technology. I remember when I first got introduced to programming in high school at Grade 11 during my Computer Applications Technology (CAT) class, I was amazed at how I could create something from nothing using just code, that was then and there I knew I wanted to pursue a career in technology.

In high school I was a shy and quiet kid but my love for entrepreneurship forced me to learn how to get out of my box. in Grade 10 I joined an entrepreneurship programme called iValue by Valued Citizens in partnership with STRATE, the programme was a 3 year programme where they taught us everything regarding business and entrepreneurship and thats where my second love started. So if i wasnt going to be in tech I wouldve dabbled in Business hence when I was awarded the opportunity to be mentored by TL Sekhoahla who is the owner of MTR VISION 30, I didnt think twice but took the opportunity with both hands.
